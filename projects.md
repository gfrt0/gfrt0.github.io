    <h2>spese fiscali</h2>
    <p>Searchable, filterable cross-edition database of Italian <em>spese fiscali</em> (tax expenditures). Covers Tavola 1 of every <em>Rapporto annuale sulle spese fiscali</em> from RSF 2016 through RSF 2024 — roughly five thousand <em>spese fiscali erariali</em> across nine editions, with a stable cross-edition ID so the same measure can be tracked over time. Each row carries identifying number, <em>norma di riferimento</em> with Normattiva deep-link, descrizione, tributo and categoria fiscale, natura, termine di vigenza, three-year financial effects, beneficiari, source-PDF page link, and governo attributions.</p>
